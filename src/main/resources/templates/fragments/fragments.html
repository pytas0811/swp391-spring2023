How to booking on BusGo

01

Access

Access to the system at BusGo. Then search for the journey presented in the Find Section.

02

Book ticket

Select a trip and book by clicking on the fare section to see the total invoice to be paid, then view the trip specifics in the details area and select BOOK.

03

Fill information

To proceed with the ticket purchase, provide the following information: customer information, contact information, and luggage information, then click Continue.

04

Payment

Choose a payment method and follow the on-screen instructions. A confirmation email for your order will be sent by BusGo.
